Entropy here is estimated as length × log2(character pool), then penalized for dictionary matches, keyboard runs, and repeated characters — the standard classroom approximation, not a full guessing-model attack.

Crack time assumes an offline attacker hashing at 10 billion guesses per second, a realistic figure for a leaked, poorly-hashed database.
